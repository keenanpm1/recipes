Ingredients:
2 cups uncooked lentils
1 cup grated cheese
1 cup bread cumbs or matzo meal
1/2 cup balsamic vinegar
2 table spoons worchestershire sauce
2 table spoons butter
2 table spoons coconut oil
2 large zuchinni chopped
1 large onion chopped
2-3 stalks of celery chopped
1 red bell pepper
2-3 table spoons olive oil
2-3 cloves garlic minced
2 eggs beaten
salt and pepper to taste

Directions:
1.  Chop vegetables, drizzle with olive oil, salt and pepper to taste, bake at 425 for 30 minutes.  Add butter and coconut oil and bake for another 30 minutes.  Do not stur!
2.  While baking bring water to boil, add lentils and reduce heat to simmer for 20 minutes.
3.  Mash lentils in a bowl, add cheese, matzo meal, balsamic vineger, worchestershire sauce and minced garlic.
4.  When vegetables are done add them to the mix and combine.
5.  When mixture is a little cool add the beaten eggs and combine.
6.  Bake the mixture in a large pan or multiple loaf pans at 350 for 1 hr.
